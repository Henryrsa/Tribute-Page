Rikhado Makhado

AKA

RIKY RICK (BossZonke)

Ricky Rick's Life and Career Timeline:

  • 1987 – Born as Rikhado Makhado on 20 July in KwaMashu, a township near Durban, South Africa.
  • 2009-2010– Enters the South African music scene by releasing mixtapes such as The Comeback Kid and Last Summer with Da L.E.S.
  • 2013 – Marries Bianca Naidoo after three years of dating. Bianca had a daughter from a previous relationship, and they later welcomed a son together.
  • 2014 – Releases hit single Fok Julle Naaiers (Nafukwa), which wins the MTV Africa Music Award for Video of the Year.
  • 2015 – Releases his debut studio album Family Values, featuring guest appearances from Cassper Nyovest, Okmalumkoolkat, Zano, and Black Motion. The album goes platinum, selling over 50,000 units.
  • 2016 – Releases hit single Sidlukotini, furthering his reputation for fashion and style in addition to his music. Wins Best Hit Single at the Metro FM Music Awards.
  • 2017 – Releases Scooby Snacks EP, featuring tracks like Oh Lord, Bandz Over Fans, and Family (featuring Frank Casino).
  • 2018 – Announces his own music and fashion festival, Cotton Fest, which becomes a major cultural event for South African youth. It debuts in February 2019.
  • 2020 – Opens up about his mental health and struggles with suicidal thoughts and substance abuse in an interview with Yanga Chief on Lab Live.
  • 2022 – Tragically passes away on 23 February in Johannesburg at the age of 34. The cause of death is reported as suicide after a long battle with depression.
